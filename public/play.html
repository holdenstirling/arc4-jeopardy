📖 How to Play

The Basics

Teams take turns picking a category and point value. The host reads the clue — teams buzz in to answer. Correct? Win the points. Wrong? Lose them.

Scoring

  • Click a dollar amount on the board to open the clue
  • Press Space to reveal the answer
  • Press 1–8 to award that team the points
  • Press Shift+1–8 to deduct points from a team

Daily Double

One hidden clue is a Daily Double — the answering team wagers up to their total score (or $1,000 minimum). Only that team may answer.

Final Jeopardy

When all clues are answered (or click 🏁 Final), every team secretly wagers before hearing the final clue. Everyone writes their answer — host reveals and adjusts scores.

Timer

Click ⏱ to cycle through OFF → 15s → 30s → 1m → 2m. Timer starts automatically when a clue opens.

Streaks 🔥

Teams earn streak badges for 3+ consecutive correct answers. Get on a roll\!
